    Emblenium

    Introducing "Emblenium"! Immerse yourself in a post-futuristic medieval world with this thrilling multidirectional rogue-lite shooter. The story revolves around Arya, a cheerful and tender-hearted young girl on a quest for answers and redemption in her small village. However, this world is plagued by dangers caused by the mysterious substance known as Emblenium, which moves through electricity and has decimated the population.

    "Emblenium" is not just an action game; it also features elements of a dating simulator, farming, and fishing. Explore a wide range of entertaining mechanics, such as artifacts that grant you benefits at the cost of negative consequences. This game represents my greatest achievement as a developer and served as the catalyst for my journey into programming seven years ago. Furthermore, it became my final project for my video game design degree.

    Prepare yourself for an immersive experience filled with emotions, challenges, and impactful decisions. "Emblenium" will take you on an unforgettable journey that combines action, romance, and exploration in a unique setting. Discover the power of Emblenium and unveil the secrets of this captivating story!

    Get ready for the ultimate adventure with "Lone Pilot"! Take control of a brave axolotl pilot and guide their ship through a storm of missiles, projectiles, and deadly hazards as you attempt to defeat a supreme boss.

    This exciting action game was developed by myself, Rodrigo Saguer Pacheco, and my two team members for our second university project. I was responsible for the programming and technical art, all made in Unity, while my colleagues took care of the modeling and music.

    We are proud to say that our game was selected as a finalist in the Student-developed Games for VGChile in 2019, and that we were able to exhibit it at Festigames, a Chilean video game exhibition.
